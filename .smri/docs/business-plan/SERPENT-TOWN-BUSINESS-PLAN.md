## Risk Analysis

### Technical Risks

**Risk:** Platform downtime  
**Mitigation:** Cloudflare 99.9% SLA, instant rollback, staging tests  
**Impact:** Low (infrastructure proven)

**Risk:** Payment processing failures  
**Mitigation:** Stripe reliability, webhook retry logic, manual reconciliation  
**Impact:** Low (Stripe 99.99% uptime)

### Market Risks

**Risk:** Low conversion rates  
**Mitigation:** Tutorial system, A/B testing, continuous optimization  
**Impact:** Medium (mitigated by education)

**Risk:** Shipping mortality  
**Mitigation:** Local pickup first, then vetted carriers, insurance  
**Impact:** Medium (start local)

### Regulatory Risks

**Risk:** CITES compliance (exotic species)  
**Mitigation:** Only sell domestically bred, legal species, proper documentation  
**Impact:** Low (ball pythons legal, bred domestically)

**Risk:** Animal welfare regulations  
**Mitigation:** Veterinary partnerships, health certificates, follow best practices  
**Impact:** Low (established protocols)

### Operational Risks

**Risk:** Inventory management  
**Mitigation:** Start with breeding stock, partner breeders, pre-orders  
**Impact:** Low (flexible model)

**Risk:** Customer support overload  
**Mitigation:** Self-service encyclopedia, automated responses, scale gradually  
**Impact:** Low (manageable volume)
